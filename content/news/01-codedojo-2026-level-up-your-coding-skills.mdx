CodeDojo is back! After the overwhelming success of last year's sessions,
Motion-U is launching CodeDojo 2026 — a structured programme designed to
take members from foundational coding concepts to building real-world,
deployable applications.

## What's New This Year?

The 2026 curriculum introduces three new tracks: Web Fundamentals
(HTML, CSS, JavaScript), Modern Frontend (React, Tailwind, Next.js),
and API Development (Node.js, Express, database integration). Each
track runs for 6 weeks with weekly hands-on sessions and a capstone
project.

What hasn't changed is the community-first approach. Every session
includes pair programming, code reviews by senior members, and an
open Slack channel for questions at any hour. You're never coding alone.

## How to Join

CodeDojo 2026 is open exclusively to Motion-U members. Sessions begin
on 28 June 2026, every Friday at 3 PM in the KICT lab. Bring your
laptop, your curiosity, and at least one specific project goal you want
to achieve by the end of the programme.

Spots are limited to 30 participants per track to keep mentor ratios
healthy. Register through the Motion-U portal — first come, first served.
