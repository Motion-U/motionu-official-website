Athena, Motion-U's very own AI assistant, has received a major update.
If you haven't talked to her lately — now is the time. Built by club
members and powered by the latest language models, Athena is designed
to be your first stop for anything Motion-U.

## What Changed

The most visible improvement: Athena now handles multi-turn conversations.
You can ask a follow-up question and she remembers context — no more
repeating yourself. Her knowledge base has also grown beyond Motion-U
specifics to include general academic guidance, study tips, and
programming help.

Under the hood, the team has reworked the retrieval pipeline to cut
response time by nearly half. Answers feel snappier, the tone is warmer,
and she's now available 24/7 at athena.motionukict.com.

## Built by Members, for Members

What makes Athena special isn't the technology — it's that she was built
by fellow Motion-U members. The project started as a hackathon idea two
semesters ago and has grown into one of the club's flagship products.
Every feature you see today was designed by students who learned to build
it as they went. That alone should tell you what joining this club can
unlock.
