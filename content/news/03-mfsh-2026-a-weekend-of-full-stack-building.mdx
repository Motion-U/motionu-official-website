**Modern Full-Stack Hero (MFSH) 2026** wrapped up last weekend, and by every measure, it was our biggest and most impactful workshop yet. Over 20 participants from across **International Islamic University Malaysia** spent two intensive days building a real, end-to-end web application — guided closely by senior members of **Motion-U**.

From fundamentals to deployment-ready projects, MFSH 2026 was designed to turn learners into builders.

## The Format

**Day One** focused on building strong foundations. Participants started with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, then gradually transitioned into the basics of **Vue.js** — understanding components, reactivity, and project structure. By the end of the first day, teams were already assembling interactive interfaces and gaining confidence in modern frontend workflows.

**Day Two** went deeper into full-stack development. Participants continued with advanced Vue concepts and integrated **PocketBase** as the backend — handling authentication, data storage, and APIs with a lightweight and developer-friendly setup. By the final session, each team had a functional application backed by a real database.

In addition to the core curriculum, MFSH 2026 introduced **special sessions on agentic coding**, where participants explored how AI-assisted and agent-driven workflows can accelerate development, improve productivity, and change the way developers build software.

The energy throughout the workshop was electric. Participants stayed well past 7 PM on both days — not out of obligation, but pure excitement. Ideas kept flowing, features kept shipping, and no one wanted to stop.

## What Participants Said

> *“I came in knowing only HTML and CSS. I left understanding Vue, PocketBase, and how a real full-stack app comes together. The mentors explained things in a way that finally made everything click.”*\
> — Aisyah, First-Year CS Student
